PART A — Public / Logged-out Pages

Home (/)

Hero: full-bleed dark/black background image with headline "Let's Find Home". Hero contains a "Search for Homes" input + SEARCH button, plus SELL YOUR HOME and SEE HOME VALUE CTAs. Below hero: featured/area sections, agent intro, footer. Live chat bubble bottom-right.

Search Listings (/search)

Map + results layout. Filter controls open as popups: BEDS (min/max popover), MORE (full filter panel). Clicking a listing card navigates to the listing detail page. Map is interactive (One Key MLS data).

Listing Detail (/search/<state>/<city>/<type>/<slug>)

Sections: photo/overview header with SAVE (heart) control, Overview, Payment Calculator (mortgage), Market Insights. SAVE requires login and adds to a Collection.

Authentication Screens (Logged-out UI)

Login and registration are a single Bootstrap modal (#sign-up-modal) opened via the header "Sign In" / "Sign Up" triggers — not standalone pages (/login returns 410). The modal has two states:

Register — "Sign Up for Free"

  • Subtext: "Create an account to save properties, receive alerts about new listings, view comparables, and obtain monthly market reports."
  • Buttons (stacked, full-width): CONTINUE WITH GOOGLE (blue), CONTINUE WITH FACEBOOK (blue), CONTINUE WITH APPLE (black).
  • Field: Email (placeholder "Enter email").
  • Primary button: CONTINUE WITH EMAIL (black).
  • Footer link: "Already have an account? Sign In".
  • Legal: "By continuing you agree to the terms of service and conditions." + Privacy Policy | Terms & Conditions links.

Login — "Welcome back!"

  • Subtext: "We've missed you! Please enter your details."
  • Social buttons: Google (blue), Facebook (blue), Apple (black) — same as register.
  • Fields: Email ("Enter email"), Password (masked).
  • Link: Forgot your password? (blue).
  • Primary button: SIGN IN (black, full-width).
  • Footer link: "Don't have an account? Sign Up".
  • Consent text (verbatim, abbreviated): express written consent to receive marketing communications via email/calls from Levan Tsiklauri; SMS consent ("reply STOP to opt out"); links to Terms & Conditions and Privacy Policy.
Design of both modals: White card, ~380px wide, centered over dimmed page. Grey heading (Lato), grey body text (#808080). Close "×" top-right. Consistent black primary CTAs; social buttons keep brand colors.
